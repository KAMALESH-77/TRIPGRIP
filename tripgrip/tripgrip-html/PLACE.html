Varkala, Kerala

Varkala is a coastal town in Kerala, known for its stunning cliffs adjacent to the Arabian Sea. It offers a perfect blend of beaches, temples, and serene landscapes.

Goa

Goa is India's smallest state known for its pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich Portuguese heritage. It's a hotspot for tourists seeking sun, sand, and fun.

Cochin, Kerala

Cochin, also known as Kochi, is a major port city in Kerala. Known for its historical significance, vibrant culture, and beautiful backwaters.

Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Manali is a picturesque hill station in the Himalayas, known for its snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and adventure activities like trekking and skiing.

Darjeeling, West Bengal

Darjeeling, known as the "Queen of the Hills," is famous for its tea plantations, panoramic views of the Himalayas, and the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.

Ooty, Tamil Nadu

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills. Known for its tea gardens, beautiful lakes, and pleasant weather.
